About Me

 

I'm a software developer and mainly specialise in web applications, based in Glenavy, Northern Ireland. I currently work for a great software conulstancy based in Hillsborough called Tascomi.

Personal Statement

 

I have twelve years experience in web to database integration and transaction based web application development. I am an innovative self-starter, motivated by new challenges and an accomplished team leader with a professional and dynamic approach to the industry.

Why swmcc?

 

In my first ever development job I had to have an account on a linux machine. These were the hey days of the dot-com boom so there were several people named Stephen already there. Steve was gone as was Stevie and Stephen. To save being called Steven or Stevo I decided on my initials SWM. Over time this got filled out to be my proper initals swmcc as McCullough really should be shorted to mcc. Also SWM in internet speak means Single White Male and is very popular on social sites - swmcc isn't that popular. Also you can get a .cc domain - so it works out well.
