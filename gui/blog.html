Why I no longer host my own blog

News, Ramblings • 31st Feb, 1986• 1932 Comments

 

Hosting your own blog is a thankless task. I have went through several iterations of hosting my own then hosting on a third party provider. I have tried various systems - wordpress, moveable type, jekyll and expression engine to name but a few. Each has their own pros and cons. Hell, I even wrote my own at one stage.

So in the last twleve years I have come to the conclusion that hosting your own blog is a fools game. Why would I bother with the resources hosting a blog takes up? Others do it much better than me with more features and better security. I decided on wordpress - mainy cos it does what I want it do without any fuss and secondly they have a nice hosting option. So if you want to see what I talk about go to swmcc.wordpress.com.

At some point in the future I might use this page to load the most recent articles from my blog. I also need to retrieve my articles from my archives when I hosted my own blog (in its various guises) and put them directly into the wordpress archive. At the minute they are just seperate sites - old.swm.cc and bilbo.swm.cc. However I am having much more fun playing with my current projects.
