Built under the skillful gorilla brand RoadClient is an app that enables remote teams in several sectors to quote for jobs (in their sector), CRM management, invoice raising and marketing.

The solution offers a secure web application that is complemented with a mobile app.

The app entered private beta for a period of six months. However it failed to gain any traction so it was shutdown.
