I have decided that I want to do at least 1km in that 5min time period. It can be easily done, and is a sufficent warm up. I didn't want to do too much cardio stuff today, mostly weights.

5mins @ level 8 - cross trainer (1.1km)
10 * 3 @ 15kg Bicep curl - free weights.
10 * 3 @ 30kg Bicep curl - machine weight.
10 * 3 @ 42kg Lateral raise - machine weight.
10 * 3 @ 50kg Tricep machine that stays beside the Lateral Raise.
10mins on the stairmaster - various levels from 8 - 12. 2mins at level 8 taken two at a time.
25 * 4 @ 40kg stomach crunch (that is gonna hoit.. I can feel it).

That was it. Was only 45mins, but I thought with the run this morning it was adequate. I also weight myself, am down about .75kg from Thursday, and as it was in the afternoon I think it was an accurate measurement :)
